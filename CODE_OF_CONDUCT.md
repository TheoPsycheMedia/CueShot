# Code of Conduct

CueShot should be a useful, respectful project for people building and improving macOS tooling.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps the project:

- Be kind, direct, and specific.
- Assume good intent while still asking for evidence.
- Keep critique focused on the work.
- Respect privacy, especially around screenshots, screen capture, and user data.
- Help newer contributors understand the codebase without gatekeeping.

Examples of behavior that does not belong here:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sexualized, violent, discriminatory, or hostile language.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests.
- Pressuring maintainers to accept unsafe capture or privacy behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject PRs, or block participants whose behavior harms the project. Serious privacy or safety concerns can be escalated privately through the security reporting path in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
