Meet CueShot: a tiny macOS capture tool built for Codex.
Open the menu bar control, press Arm, then click or drag.
Use Element for exact bounds, Selection for a fast crop, or Area for a manual rectangle.
In Element mode, CueShot follows the UI under your cursor and locks the exact control you mean.
Here, a settings toggle is selected cleanly, without grabbing the whole window.
Then CueShot saves a local PNG, copies it instantly, and shows a preview you can paste or drag into Codex.
It stays local, explains permissions, and helps builders show exactly what they mean.
