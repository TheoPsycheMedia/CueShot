# CueShot Promo Demo Storyboard

Runtime target: 36 seconds. Format: 1920 x 1080, 30 fps.

## Scene 1: The Pain And Promise

Time: 0.0-4.2

Visual: App icon, CueShot wordmark, reticle pulse, compact headline.

Voiceover: "Meet CueShot: a tiny macOS capture tool built for Codex."

On-screen copy: "Point at the UI detail. Drop it into Codex."

## Scene 2: The Activation Loop

Time: 4.2-10.2

Visual: Coded floating control, three step pills, cursor press on Arm.

Voiceover: "Click the menu bar icon, show the floating control, press Arm, then click or drag."

On-screen copy: "Menu bar -> Arm -> Click or drag"

## Scene 3: The Capture Types

Time: 10.2-16.4

Visual: Coded main window, mode rail emphasis, privacy-safe mock target, labels for Element, Selection, Area, Window, Screen.

Voiceover: "Use Element for exact Accessibility bounds, Selection for a fast crop, or Area for a manual rectangle."

On-screen copy: "Exact when available. Estimated when faster. Manual when needed."

## Scene 4: Element Selection Scenario

Time: 16.4-22.2

Visual: Coded Product Settings mock app. Cursor selects a single toggle element, exact-bounds ring locks to the row, and a readout confirms the target.

Voiceover: "In Element mode, CueShot follows the UI under your cursor and locks the exact control you mean. Here, a settings toggle is selected cleanly, without grabbing the whole window."

On-screen copy: "Capture the exact control."

## Scene 5: The Handoff

Time: 22.2-29.4

Visual: Coded output inspector, last capture chip, Save As / Copy PNG / Codex destination highlights.

Voiceover: "CueShot saves a PNG locally, sends to Codex when available, and falls back to the clipboard."

On-screen copy: "Local PNG. Codex handoff. Clipboard fallback."

## Scene 6: GitHub Close

Time: 29.4-36.0

Visual: Coded Settings and onboarding panels, icon lockup, final CTA.

Voiceover: "It stays local, explains its permissions, and gives builders a cleaner way to show what they mean."
